2023 Transfer Window

The 2023 English football summer transfer window runs from 14 June to 1 September 2023. Players without a club may be signed at any time, clubs may sign players on loan dependent on their league's regulations, and clubs may sign a goalkeeper on an emergency loan if they have no registered senior goalkeeper available.

Jude Bellingam

Known for his complete skillset, as well as his maturity and leadership qualities, Bellingham had become the youngest ever Bundesliga captain during his time at Borussia Dortmund, leading out the German side while he was still just 19 years old.
