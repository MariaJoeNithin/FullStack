One of my Fav destination is Kodailkanal.Kodaikanal is known for its rich flora. Of the big trees, cypress, eucalyptus and acacia are the dominant varieties. Pear trees are numerous and the fruits are of high quality.

2.Ooty

Ooty is popularly known as Queen of Hill Stations.
It is also known as the Blue Mountains and is one of the most beautiful hill stations located in South India. It is one of the finest summer resort where the climate is chill and cool even in summer. Ooty is located about 7,500 feet above Sea level.
